Computer evolution
- The First Generation, 1944 - 1958, the era of Vacuum Tubes
Computers use vacuum tubes as the main component. It uses high power, so there is a problem with heating. Order using machine language (Machine Code or Machine Language) computers are large. Computers of this era include ASCC (Automatic Sequence Controlled Calculator or Mark I), ENIAC (Electronic Numerical And Calculator), EDVAC, IBM 650, UNIVAC I, etc.
- The Second Generation, 1959 - 1964, Transistor
Computers use transistors as the main component. Can operate using high-level languages such as FORTRAN (Formula Translation), BASIC (Beginners All-purpose Symbolic Instruction Code), COBAL (Common Business Oriented), etc. Some machines have adopted a magnetic ring (Magnetic Core) is used as a component. Examples of computers in this era are IBM 1401, IBM 7000, CD 3600, etc.
- The Third Generation, 1964 - 1974, the era of integrated circuits (IC: Integrated Circuits).
Computers in this era will use integrated circuits (IC: Integrated Circuit) as the main component. which is crystalline silicon that inside can be made into a transistor And other components that can be combined into circuits for performing various tasks by bringing in the IC to create the CPU, Memory and other components of the computer. Which computers in this era include IBM 360, UNIVAC 9400, etc.
- The Fourth Generation Computer, 1975, the era of large-scale integrated circuits (LSI: Large Scale Integrated Circuits).
In this era, small electronic devices were created as silicon crystals that consisted of enormous number of sub-circuits, as if combining many ICs, called large-scale integrated circuits (LSI: Large Scale Integrated Circuits). New LSIs for use as CPUs until developed into very high-capacity integrated circuits (VLSI: Very Large Scale Integration or Very Large Scale Integrated Circuits) such as Micro Processors. Computers in this era include IBM 370, IBM 4311, NEC 300, IBM/PC and many others
- The Fifth Generation Computer, The era of very large integrated circuits.
An agency was established to find a way to develop computers. VLSI has been developed to be more efficient, e.g. for CPU cores. And computers in this era are smaller and more efficient. There have been experiments and research to find out how to make computers as intelligent as humans, such as artificial intelligence (AI: Artificial Intelligence), etc., and have put various systems in the same chip (SoC: System on Chip) most devices. Able to connect to the Internet(IoT : Internet of Thing) and have AI to help manage
- The Sixth Generation Computer
Because in order to increase the performance of the computer, the size of the transistor must be reduced. and increase the number of transistors in the processor chip But humans can't produce smaller than the present. Therefore, a quantum computer was created.
